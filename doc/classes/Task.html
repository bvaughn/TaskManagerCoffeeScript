    Class: Task

    Defined in: ./source/task.coffee
    Inherits: EventDispatcher

    Overview

    A Task is any operation that can be started and completed. A Task may be a self-contained operation or it may be a composite of many such other Tasks.

    To create a usable Task, extend this class and override the customRun(), customReset(), and customInterrupt() methods. Your Task should call taskComplete() or taskError() upon completion.
